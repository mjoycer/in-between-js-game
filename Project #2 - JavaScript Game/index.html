♦ ♣ In-Between ♠ ♥

The computer will reveal two cards. The player will decide to bet any amount. The computer will show a third card. If the third card is in-between the two cards shown, the player wins double of the amount of coins they bet. If not, then they lose the number of coins they bet. The game is over when the player has no more coins. The value of the cards are as follows:
A= 1, J=11, Q=12, K=13, and the number cards will have the same value as their number.
